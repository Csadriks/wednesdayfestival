Festival Timeline

9 August 2023

14:00

Participants
Alfred Gough and Miles Millar

The opening speech of the festival will be given by the creators of the series, Alfred Gough and Miles Millar, from whom you can talk about anything related to the series. Alfred and Miles will also report on where the basic idea for the series came from and how they dreamed and imagined the story.

After the opening speech Jenna Ortega will give a dance performance as Wednesday, where she will revive the dance seen in the series.

9 August 2023

17:30

Lady Gaga concert

Lady Gaga was so captivated by this series that she decided she could make a good party out of it, and she would make a special production for her viral music. 

9 August 2023

21:30

Bloody techno


A terrifyingly good party will be created in the bloody techno hall by the surprise dj. For now, it's a secret who he will be, and it will be possible to find out on the spot!

You can find answers to your questions so far here, as the creators of the series will be present, as well as Jenna Ortega personally. Lady Gaga, a very iconic person, ends up blowing up the party, which will be followed by a terrifyingly good techno party.
